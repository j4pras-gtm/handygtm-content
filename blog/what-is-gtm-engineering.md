GTM Engineering sits between strategy and execution. Strategy decides what to do. GTM Engineering decides how the work actually gets built, run, and improved as a system.

Without it, GTM lives in slide decks, scattered tools, and one-off campaigns. With it, GTM looks more like a product: versioned workflows, observable outcomes, and a team that ships changes weekly instead of quarterly.

If you're a founder or operator running on a small team, GTM Engineering is the cheapest unfair advantage you can build for yourself this year.
